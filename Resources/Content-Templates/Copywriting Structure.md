## The "Universal" Structure

### Hook
Hook people to read your post. Create a Pattern Interrupt. Stop their scroll.
-

### Key Points
Explain 1 to 3 big ideas that would help your reader.
-

### Call to Action
Make them act.
-

---
## PAS Structure

### Problem
State the problem for your reader.
-

### Aggravate
Make it vivid inside their brain by depicting how it negatively affects their life.
-

### Solution
Give them a concrete solution to fix the pain and move toward their desired state.
-

---
## What, Why, How

### What
State the topic you're talking.
-

### Why
Explain why it's important.
-

### How
Explain how to do it.
-

---
## APAG Structure

### Attention
Grab your readers' attention by stating their problems and their consequences.
-

### Perspective
Give your own, unique perspective on this subject. Explain why their beliefs until now were false and what they should believe instead.
Add examples, numbers, and social proof to illustrate your perspective.
-

### Advantage
Explain the benefits of your unique perspective.
-

### Gamify
Give a concrete step-by-step plan to move away from pain and toward a desirable result.
-

---
## AIDA Structure

### Awareness
Grab their attention in less than 2 seconds with a hook.
-

### Interest
Talk to the prospect about his problem and the solution and shift his beliefs (Move him from point A to B)
-

### Desire
Describe your product/service and state it's benefits
-

### Action
Removing the last blockages to take action
-
