### 1) Finding Your Angle
**Do a YouTube search on your topic: has it been covered before? From what perspective?**
-
**What questions or concerns come to mind when you think about your topic? What don't you understand about your topic?**
-
**Are there any oddities in the timeline that puzzle you?**
-
**Is your topic an exception in its category? Why?**
-
**Do you have a concept in mind to analyze your topic?**
-
**Is there a parallel between your topic and another example? Why do you think of this parallel?**
-

### 2) Reinforcing Your Angle
**Can you use the negativity bias to analyze your topic?**
-
**Can your video be a revelation?**
-
**Are there counterintuitive elements that coexist in your topic?**
-
**Does your topic present contradictions? Which ones? How are they articulated?**
-
**Can your video stage a spectacle or a storytelling?**
-
**Can your video stage a promise?**
