# Creating 7 Days of Viral Twitter Content in 60 Minutes

Type : #prompt
Subject : #ai #creativity
Status : #active
## Step 1: Organize Your Source Content
- Gather your newsletters, articles, or other long-form content
- These will serve as the raw material for your tweets

## Step 2: Open an AI Chat
- Open a new AI chat session (Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI assistant)
- If your tool supports document referencing, add your source content as context

## Step 3: Add Context
- Provide the AI with:
  - Your recent newsletter(s) or articles (paste or attach)
  - A description of your writing style/voice
  - Optionally: examples of your best-performing tweets

## Step 4: Use the Optimized Prompt

```
You are an experienced twitter ghostwriter with more than 20 years of experience who has written thousands of viral tweets. Your task is to create tweets following specific templates using the ideas from my newsletter content.

For each tweet:
1. Extract a valuable insight or idea from my newsletter
2. Format it according to one of the template structures below
3. Maintain my authentic voice and style
4. Make sure each tweet stands alone as valuable content

Create 7 different tweets, each using a different structure from this list:
1. Contrarian take / hot take
2. Before/After transformation
3. Specific number + benefit (e.g., "3 ways to...")
4. Question that triggers curiosity
5. Actionable tip with clear outcome
6. Mistake to avoid + better alternative
7. Bold statement + supporting insight

For each tweet:
- Write it in plain text format
- Include the structure name you used as a label
- Ensure it's under 280 characters
- Use strategic line breaks for readability
- Optimize for engagement while maintaining authenticity

The tweets should feel like they were written by me, just formatted specifically for Twitter's environment.
```

## Step 5: Review and Refine
- Examine each generated tweet for accuracy and voice
- Ask the AI to refine any tweets that don't quite capture your style
- Ensure each tweet accurately represents your ideas

## Step 6: Schedule Your Content
- Copy the finalized tweets to your preferred scheduling tool (e.g., Typefully)
- Space them out over the week for consistent presence

## Pro Tips
- Aim for 70% automated content, 30% spontaneous engagement
- Create a document analyzing your writing style by asking AI to review your previous tweets
- Repeat this process weekly to maintain consistency without burnout
