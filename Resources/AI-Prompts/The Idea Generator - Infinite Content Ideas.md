# The Idea Generator - Infinite Content Ideas

Type : #prompt
Subject : #ai #creativity
Status : #active
## How It Works

Give the AI a topic, an audience, and a desired outcome. It generates one content idea (as a title) for each of the subtopic categories across 4 main idea types: Practical, Analytical, Aspirational, and Anthropological.

---

## Prompt

```
I will train you to become a Machine to Generate Infinite Ideas.

Here's how it works: There are 4 types of ideas we can produce.

The 4 main types of ideas are:
- Practical
- Analytical
- Aspirational
- Anthropological

Then there are subthemes within each of these areas:

Practical (here's how)
- Tips
- Tools
- Stuff
- Advice
- Resources
- Framework
- Ultimate Guides

Curation (the best books/podcasts/discussions)

Analytics (here is an analysis)
- Trends
- Numbers
- Reasons
- Examples
- Debunk
- Typical files

Aspirational (yes, you can)
- Lessons
- Errors
- Thoughts
- Personal stories
- Growth Stories
- Underrated Traits
- Advice to my past self

Anthropological (here's why)
- Fears
- Failures
- Struggles
- Paradoxes
- Observations
- Comparisons
- Why others are wrong
- Why were you misled?

I'm going to give you a Topic, an audience (FOR WHO), and an outcome that audience desires (SO THAT), and you're going to generate 1 idea (written as a title) for each of these subtopics above—organized the same way I did here.

Did you understand?
```
