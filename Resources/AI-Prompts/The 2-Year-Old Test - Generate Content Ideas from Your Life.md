# The 2-Year-Old Test - Generate Content Ideas from Your Life

Type : #prompt
Subject : #ai #creativity
Status : #active
> Video Reference: https://youtu.be/4FzKi4J0_Jc?si=wWJ-utdVRCI9sUrx

## How It Works

This prompt interviews you about your life over the last 2-5 years, then generates 20 content title ideas based on your answers.

---

## Prompt

```
I need a little help finding topics to write about.

I want you to learn about my life, career, experiences, etc., over the last 2-5 years so that you can suggest different topics for me to write about.

We will divide this into 2 distinct steps.

Step 1: First, you will interview me and ask me these 10 questions, one by one, giving me time to answer each one before moving on to the next one. Once I have provided the answer, ask me if I am ready for the next question, and so on:

1. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to improve your personal growth?
2. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to expand your knowledge or skills?
3. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to improve your health and general well-being?
4. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to improve your relationships with others?
5. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to improve your financial stability?
6. Over the past 2 to 5 years, what 3 new experiences have broadened your perspective on life?
7. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to push yourself out of your comfort zone?
8. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 actions have you taken to improve your creativity or pursue new hobbies?
9. Over the past 2 to 5 years, what 3 challenges have you faced and overcome, and how have they changed your life?
10. Over the last 2 to 5 years, what 3 things have you learned about yourself and your values?

Ask me if I'm ready to begin the interview, and when I agree, ask me each question in sequential order.

We need to complete this Step 1 before moving on to Step 2.

Once I have answered the 10 questions above, we will move on to Step 2 where you will generate a list of 20 content titles that I could write based on the answers you provided to me in Step 1.

Did you understand? If so, let's start with Step 1 and ask me if I'm ready to begin the interview – then ask me the 10 questions, one by one, giving me time to answer each one before moving on to the next one.
```
